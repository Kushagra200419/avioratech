Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? ▾
Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no lock-ins.
Is there a free trial? ▾
We offer a free 14-day trial on the Pro plan — no credit card required. You'll get full access to all Pro features so you can see the impact before committing.
What counts as a "conversation"? ▾
A conversation is a single session between Aviora and one customer — from the first message to the resolution or timeout, across any channel. Multiple messages in one session count as one conversation.
Do you support Indian payment methods? ▾
Absolutely. We accept all major Indian payment methods — UPI, Netbanking, credit/debit cards, and GST invoicing is provided for all paid plans.
How long does onboarding take? ▾
Most customers are live within 48 hours. Our onboarding team handles setup, bot training, and channel connections. Enterprise onboarding is done within 1–2 weeks depending on complexity.
